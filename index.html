Why This Works

The 3–4am wake-up

Histamine peaks at night and prevents your nervous system from downshifting. Box breathing activates your vagus nerve and overrides the panic signal.

When to use it

3am panic · Before getting out of bed · Any moment you feel overwhelmed · Before sleep
