Box Breathing

Inhale 4 counts · Hold 4 counts · Exhale 4 counts · Hold 4 counts. Repeat 4–6 rounds. Tap the circle to start.

Tap to start
Why This Works

The 3–4am wake-up

Histamine naturally peaks at night and prevents your nervous system from downshifting. Box breathing activates your vagus nerve and manually overrides the panic signal.

Diaphragmatic breathing

Breathe into your belly, not your chest. Put one hand on your stomach — it should rise first. This switches you from sympathetic (fight-or-flight) to parasympathetic (rest and digest).

When to use it

3am panic · Before getting out of bed · Any moment you feel overwhelmed · Before sleep · Anytime your mind won't stop

Stretching · 5–10 min
Cat/Cow x 8 slow
On hands and knees. Arch and round your spine slowly.
Breathe through every rep. No rushing.
Child's Pose 30 sec
Sit back toward heels, arms stretched forward.
Let your body melt into the floor.
Hip Flexor Stretch 20 sec each
Half kneeling on floor. Shift forward gently.
Feel the front of the hip, not the knee.
Seated Spinal Twist 20 sec each
Sit on floor, cross one leg over, twist toward it.
Exhale as you twist. Inhale to sit taller.
Done with stretching ✓
Floor Work · 2–3 rounds
Glute Bridge x 12
Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat. Drive hips up.
2-second squeeze at the top. This is your most important move.
Bird Dog x 8 each side
On hands and knees. Extend opposite arm and leg slowly.
Keep your hips level. Slow is the whole point.
Dead Bug x 8 each side
Lie on back, arms up, knees at 90°. Lower opposite arm and leg.
Low back stays flat on the floor the entire time.
Knee Push-Up x 8–10
Hands wider than shoulders, knees on floor. Lower your chest.
Body stays in a straight line from knees to head.
Wall Push-Up x 10–12
Hands on wall at chest height. Lean in and push back.
Start here if knee push-ups feel like too much today.
Done with floor work ✓
Standing · Optional add-on
Bodyweight Squat x 10–12
Feet shoulder-width. Sit back like there's a chair behind you.
Don't lock your knees at the top.
Standing March x 20 steps
Lift knees to hip height, slow and controlled.
Hold onto a wall if needed. Stay tall.
Banded Lateral Walk x 10 each way
Band just above knees. Step side to side, stay low.
Only if you have a band. Skip if not.
Done with standing work ✓
